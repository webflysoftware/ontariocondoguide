Ontario Condo Guide — MVP Tech Sheet

Purpose:
Build a neutral, authoritative Ontario condo resource website designed to attract organic search traffic from condo owners, board directors, property managers, and condo corporations. The site should provide practical guidance around Ontario condo governance topics while creating a natural lead path toward ElectoSense for election, voting, proxy, quorum, and AGM-related use cases.

Primary Domain:
OntarioCondoGuide.ca

Brand Name:
Ontario Condo Guide

Positioning Line:
Practical resources for condo boards, owners, and property managers.

Primary Objective:
Launch a useful resource site that can begin ranking for Ontario condo-related searches and quietly generate qualified leads for condo voting and governance software.

Primary Audience:
Ontario condo board directors
Condo owners trying to understand meetings, voting, proxies, quorum, and board processes
Property managers looking for simple resources to share with boards or owners
Condo corporations preparing for AGMs, elections, special meetings, proxy collection, or owner votes

Brand Feel:
The site should feel like a serious independent resource for Ontario condo governance.
It should feel practical, plain-language, and useful.
It should not feel like a SaaS landing page, lead farm, law firm, fake regulator, or government imitation.
It should build trust through clarity, specificity, useful templates, and consistent Ontario condo terminology.
It should prioritize helpfulness first and lead generation second.

Authority Positioning:
The site should act as a practical guide to common Ontario condo board and owner processes.
It should focus on explaining how things generally work, what boards should prepare, what owners may encounter, and what tools or templates can help.
It should avoid claiming legal authority.
It should avoid sounding like official government guidance.
It should include clear disclaimers that the content is general educational information and not legal advice.
