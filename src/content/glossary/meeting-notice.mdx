## In plain language

Owners cannot be surprised by a binding vote. The **meeting notice** tells every owner when the meeting happens, how to attend (including virtually if offered), what business is on the agenda, and includes a **proxy form** for owners' meetings.

Notice timing and content are set by the **Condominium Act** and your **declaration** and **bylaws**. Short notice or missing proxy forms can invalidate the meeting process.

## Typical contents

- Date, time, and place or electronic access instructions
- Agenda or description of business
- Proxy form and submission instructions
- Financial statements for AGMs when required
- Wording of bylaw amendments or other motions to be voted on
