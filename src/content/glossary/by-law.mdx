## In plain language

**Bylaws** (often written "by-laws" in older documents) sit below the **declaration** in the hierarchy of condo documents. They typically cover board structure, meeting procedures, notice requirements, and administrative details.

Amending a bylaw usually requires an **owners' vote** at a properly called meeting with sufficient notice. Bylaws cannot contradict the Act or declaration.

## Common bylaw topics

- Number of directors and term lengths
- Quorum and meeting adjournment rules
- Notice periods for owners' meetings
- Electronic voting or hybrid meeting authorization
