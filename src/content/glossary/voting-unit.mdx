## In plain language

In most Ontario condos, each **voting unit** equals **one vote** at owners' meetings — regardless of how many people co-own the unit. The total number of voting units is the starting point for **quorum** math and election tallies.

Parking lockers or separate parcels may or may not be separate voting units depending on the **declaration**. Always confirm your corporation's unit register.

## Why it matters

- Quorum = a percentage of all voting units represented
- Proxies represent one voting unit each
- Double-counting the same unit invalidates quorum calculations
