## In plain language

A **hybrid meeting** combines a physical location with online access — owners or directors can join from the meeting room or by video. Hybrid formats can improve **quorum** and owner access if remote participation counts toward attendance under your rules.

Corporations must confirm **bylaws** and **notice** authorize hybrid meetings and describe how owners register and vote remotely.

## Planning considerations

- Reliable audiovisual so remote participants hear and are heard
- Clear registration for in-person and remote attendees
- Voting method that works for all channels
- Backup plan if the virtual platform fails
