## In plain language

Before owners can **elect directors**, candidates must be **nominated** according to the corporation's rules. Nominations may occur during a published nomination period before the meeting or from the floor at the meeting if permitted.

Candidates typically submit **written consent** to serve and confirm they meet eligibility requirements in the declaration or bylaws.

## Common requirements

- Owner status or other qualifications defined in governing documents
- Written consent to serve if elected
- Nomination by another owner or self-nomination if allowed
- Disclosure of conflicts relevant to board service
