## Before nominations open

- [ ] Confirm election date and number of open director positions
- [ ] Review eligibility rules in declaration and bylaws
- [ ] Prepare nomination form or written nomination requirements
- [ ] Prepare candidate consent and disclosure form
- [ ] Set nomination period dates and publish in meeting notice

## For each nomination received

- [ ] Candidate name and unit number recorded
- [ ] Nominator is an eligible owner if required by documents
- [ ] Seconding nominator obtained if required
- [ ] Written consent to serve signed and dated
- [ ] Candidate meets age, occupancy, or other qualification rules
- [ ] Conflicts of interest disclosed (related contracts, management ties)
- [ ] Candidate biography collected if corporation distributes statements
- [ ] Acknowledgment sent to candidate and nominator

## Closing the nomination period

- [ ] Nomination period closed at stated deadline
- [ ] Final candidate list verified by board or management
- [ ] Determine acclamation vs contested election for each position
- [ ] Publish candidate list to owners within required timeframe
- [ ] Prepare ballots or electronic ballot configuration for contested seats

## Floor nominations at meeting (if permitted)

- [ ] Chair confirms floor nomination rules before election item
- [ ] Verify candidate consent obtained before vote
- [ ] Update ballot or voting system if floor candidate added
