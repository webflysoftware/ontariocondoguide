## Before you start

Confirm your meeting date, location or virtual platform, and whether elections or special business are on the agenda.

## Checklist

- [ ] Confirm meeting date and location
- [ ] Draft preliminary agenda
- [ ] Prepare notice package (placeholder)
- [ ] Confirm quorum requirements
- [ ] Arrange proxy collection process
- [ ] Prepare candidate nomination process (if applicable)
- [ ] Test electronic voting setup (if applicable)
