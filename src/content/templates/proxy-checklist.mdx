## Owner: before you submit

- [ ] Proxy form is the one included in the official meeting notice package
- [ ] Meeting date and corporation name match the notice
- [ ] Owner name(s) match corporation records for the unit
- [ ] Unit number is correct and identifies the voting unit
- [ ] Proxy holder name and contact information are complete
- [ ] Alternate proxy holder named (if desired and permitted)
- [ ] Voting instructions completed for elections and motions (if directed proxy)
- [ ] Owner signature and date are present
- [ ] Co-owner signature obtained if required by your documents
- [ ] Form submitted before the deadline in the notice
- [ ] Submitted using the delivery method specified (portal, email, mail, or management office)
- [ ] Copy retained for your records

## Manager: upon receipt

- [ ] Log receipt date and time for each proxy
- [ ] Confirm unit is not duplicated by another proxy or in-person registration
- [ ] Verify signature matches owner of record or authorized party
- [ ] Flag incomplete forms and contact owner before cut-off
- [ ] File originals or secure scans per corporation policy
- [ ] Add validated units to quorum tracking sheet

## Chair: at registration

- [ ] Cross-check registration list against proxy log
- [ ] Reject only with documented reason under applicable rules
- [ ] Ensure each proxy holder registers each unit separately
- [ ] Record total units represented for quorum declaration
