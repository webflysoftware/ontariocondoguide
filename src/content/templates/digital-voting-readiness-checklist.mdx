## Legal and document review

- [ ] Declaration and bylaws reviewed for electronic voting authority
- [ ] Bylaw or owner approval obtained if required
- [ ] Meeting notice describes electronic voting method clearly
- [ ] Legal counsel consulted for first-time or contested use

## Vendor and platform selection

- [ ] Platform supports owner authentication and audit trail
- [ ] Data residency and privacy terms reviewed
- [ ] Contract defines support on meeting day and backup procedure
- [ ] Platform handles proxies or integrates with registration process
- [ ] Cost disclosed to board and reflected in budget if applicable

## Owner communication

- [ ] Instructions sent with meeting notice (login, deadlines, help contact)
- [ ] FAQ published for owners without email or technology access
- [ ] Alternative participation path available (proxy or in-person if hybrid)
- [ ] Test login or sandbox offered to owners before voting opens

## Technical testing

- [ ] Test election or motion configured with sample units
- [ ] Backup internet and hardware plan for in-person hub
- [ ] Failover to paper ballot or adjournment procedure documented
- [ ] Support staff trained on platform admin functions
- [ ] Voting window open/close times configured correctly

## Meeting day

- [ ] Registration reconciled with electronic voter list
- [ ] Quorum tracked including electronic participants
- [ ] Scrutineer or independent observer assigned
- [ ] Results exported and saved immediately after vote closes
- [ ] Voting results record completed and signed

## Records retention

- [ ] Audit log stored with meeting minutes
- [ ] Owner data handled per privacy policy
- [ ] Results filed in corporation records
