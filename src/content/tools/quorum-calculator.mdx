## How to use

Enter your corporation's total eligible voting units, the quorum percentage required by your governing documents, and current participation counts. The calculator shows whether quorum appears met and how many additional units are needed.

## What counts toward quorum

In most Ontario condo meetings, quorum is measured by **units represented** — in person, by valid proxy, or through authorized advance or electronic voting where permitted. Your declaration and bylaws define the exact percentage and any special rules.

## Next steps if quorum is not met

If participation falls short, review the [what happens if quorum is not met](/guides/what-happens-if-quorum-not-met) guide and use the [quorum tracking sheet](/templates/quorum-tracking-sheet) to monitor outreach before the meeting.
