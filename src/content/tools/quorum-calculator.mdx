## How to use

Enter your corporation's total eligible units, quorum percentage, and current confirmed participation. The calculator shows whether quorum appears met and how many additional units may be needed.

## Notes

This tool provides a general estimate for planning purposes. Always confirm quorum rules in your declaration, bylaws, and governing documents.
