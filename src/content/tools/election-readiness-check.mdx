## How to use

Work through each item below before your election notice goes out. The tool calculates a readiness score and lists missing steps. Use it alongside your corporation's governing documents and the [condo election checklist](/templates/condo-election-checklist).

## What this covers

- Open positions and nomination process
- Voter list preparation
- Proxy and quorum planning
- Ballot format and scrutineer arrangements
- Electronic voting authorization (if applicable)

This tool provides general planning guidance, not legal advice.
