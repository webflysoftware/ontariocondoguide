## What is quorum?

Quorum is the threshold of eligible voting units that must be represented — in person, by proxy, or by other permitted means — before a meeting can conduct business.

## Why it matters

Without quorum, the corporation may need to adjourn and try again, which delays elections, approvals, and other decisions.
